The Neuro-Mechanical Framework

Welcome to The Neuro-Mechanical Animal. Within this therapeutic architecture, we honor a foundational truth: the creative animal (you) is completely mediated through neuro-mechanical pathways. Every abstract thought, complex emotion, and visceral sensation exists as a bi-directional dialogue between the central nervous system and the structural body framework.

When our physiological architecture is fully regulated, life force and intentional movement move fluidly. When the system faces chronic threat, our capacity for creativity, relational safety, and presence becomes locked into survival-driven somatic patterns.

Nervous System Regulation & Somatic Practice

Complex trauma, developmental stress, and high-stimulus modern environments fundamentally alter autonomic nervous system wiring. Stuck in states of high sympathetic activation (chronic fight or flight) or dorsal vagal immobilization (systemic shutdown and freeze), the body defaults into protective bracing patterns. This displays physically as altered breath cycles, neuromuscular holding habits, and lingering myofascial constriction.

Somatic practice is the targeted process of re-establishing somatic mapping and down-regulating hyper-aroused states. By working through a bottom-up framework, we safely resolve somatic dissociation—where the brain fragments away from real-time physical feedback—and rebuild neural safety, expanding your baseline resilience and capacity for regulation from the inside out.

Answers to Common Questions

How does somatic nervous system regulation differ from traditional talk therapy?

Traditional psychotherapy heavily prioritizes top-down cognitive processing, verbal narratives, and diagnostic insight. Somatic regulation utilizes a bottom-up framework. We prioritize structural patterns and physiological states directly. Rather than intellectualizing an experience, we work to alter the body's physical integration of defensive stress states, actively retraining the nervous system to process real-time safety signals.

Do you accept insurance?

No, commercial health insurance is not accepted for somatic coaching or enteric vitality services. If you reside in California and explicitly require formal mental health psychotherapy under standard diagnostic guidelines, you may contact me directly to discuss traditional therapeutic care pathways via my professional clinical registration channels.

What should I expect from my initial somatic mapping session?

Autonomic baseline restructuring requires consistency; we highly recommend a baseline commitment of five sessions to shift deeply grooved physiological responses. Your intake session maps your unique developmental background and somatic tracking capacity. From there, you will receive tailored somatic exercises, breathing alignment protocols, and access to relevant digital tools to begin independent nervous system tracking.
